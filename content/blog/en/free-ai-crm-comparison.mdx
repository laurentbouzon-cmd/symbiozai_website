## The "free AI CRM" promise: what it hides

In 2026, searching "free AI CRM" returns dozens of comparison articles. They all list the same tools: HubSpot Free, Zoho CRM Free, Freshsales Free, Bitrix24. Some add open-source options like SuiteCRM or Twenty.

What these comparisons don't tell you: **AI features are systematically disabled in free tiers**. That's not an oversight. It's the business model.

This guide tells you what each free tool actually does, where the AI stops, and when the cost of a free tool exceeds the cost of a paid one.

## Why CRMs have a free tier

The free tier in SaaS has a precise logic: get you into the ecosystem, store your data, make you dependent on integrations — and upsell you to paid plans when you need more.

According to [2026 CRM statistics](https://crm.org/crmland/crm-statistics), HubSpot dominates the SMB segment with 62% of installations. That dominance wasn't built on quality alone — it was built on the strategic generosity of the free tier, which serves as a Trojan horse toward Starter, Professional, and Enterprise plans.

This isn't a criticism. It's a known model. The problem is when users believe the free tier will be enough.

## Free AI CRMs in 2026: what they actually do

### HubSpot CRM Free

**What's free:** Unlimited contact management, deals, activities, basic email tracking, Gmail/Outlook integrations.

**What is NOT free:** Breeze AI — HubSpot's AI assistant — is reserved for paid plans (Starter from €15/month, Professional from €90/month). The free tier has no automatic enrichment, no AI suggestions, no lead scoring.

**Verdict:** HubSpot Free is an excellent free traditional CRM. Not a free AI CRM. For teams of under 5 people managing fewer than 200 contacts, it's a solid starting point — knowing you'll eventually pay.

**Hidden limit:** No advanced automation features without Starter. Email tracking sends a HubSpot notification to your contacts (some find this intrusive).

### Zoho CRM Free

**What's free:** 3 users maximum, contact management, leads, basic deals. Some simple automations.

**What is NOT free:** Zia — Zoho's AI assistant — starts from the Standard plan (€14/user/month). The free tier has no AI scoring, prediction, or suggestions.

**Verdict:** Suitable only for very small teams (2-3 people) with very limited deal volume. Beyond that, limitations block quickly.

### Freshsales Free

**What's free:** Contact management, basic visual pipeline, email integration.

**What is NOT free:** Freddy AI (scoring, recommendations, prediction) starts from the paid Growth plan (€9/user/month). The "Free" plan exists but is extremely limited — fewer than 3 users, no customizable pipeline.

**Verdict:** Freshsales Free is more a demo tool than a working tool. It allows you to test the interface before committing.

### Twenty (open-source)

**What's free:** Complete CRM interface, self-hosted or cloud. Modern architecture, open API, full customization.

**What is NOT free:** AI is not native — you need to integrate it via third-party connectors or build custom plugins. For a non-technical team, this is out of reach.

**Verdict:** Twenty is excellent for teams with developers who want full control over their CRM. For sales teams who want AI without configuration effort, it's not the right solution.

### SuiteCRM (open-source)

**What's free:** Complete enterprise-grade CRM, self-hosted. Sales, support, and marketing management features.

**What is NOT free:** No native AI. Integration modules exist (OpenAI, etc.) but require technical expertise and significant configuration.

**Verdict:** Powerful for organizations with IT resources. Unrealistic for an autonomous sales team that wants AI without technical intervention.

## When a free CRM makes sense

A free CRM is relevant in these specific cases:

**You're in validation stage:** Your sales team has fewer than 3 people, you're testing your go-to-market, and you don't yet have enough deal volume to justify an advanced tool. HubSpot Free or Zoho Free are appropriate.

**You have developers:** You can deploy Twenty or SuiteCRM, configure them, and maintain them. You want full control over your data without any subscription.

**Your sales cycle is very simple:** Fewer than 15 active deals per rep, short cycle (less than 2 weeks), few interactions to track. AI adds little value in this context.
